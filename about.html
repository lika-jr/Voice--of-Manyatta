About Voice of the Manyatta

Our Vision & Mission

VISION: A community where every girl is free from all forms of Gender-Based Violence (GBV) and lives in dignity and empowerment.

MISSION: To raise awareness, provide support, and empower girls and communities to end harmful cultural practices and promote gender equity.

What We Do

Health & Protection

We promote access to health services and protect girls from early marriages and abuse.

Education Support

We support safe education opportunities for rescued and vulnerable girls.

Community Awareness

We educate communities to challenge harmful traditions and foster change.

GBV Prevention

We lead efforts to stop all forms of Gender-Based Violence through advocacy.

Join Hands With Us

Whether you're an organization, an individual, or a donor — your support helps us reach more girls and protect their future. Let's end GBV together.
